Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2024

1. Information We Collect

typeshi.fun is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Anonymous chat messages (temporarily during active sessions)
  • Basic connection information (IP address, browser type)
  • Aggregated usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Monitor for abuse and violations of our terms
  • Improve user experience

3. Data Retention

We practice ephemeral data handling:

  • Chat messages are not stored after the session ends
  • Connection logs are automatically deleted after 30 days
  • No personal information is required to use our service

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal, essential cookies to maintain your chat session. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing personal information
  • Disconnect from chats at any time
  • Request information about any data we might have about you

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
